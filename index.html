A Silent Threat to Our Ecosystems

The Hidden Danger

Even when boat exteriors are meticulously cleaned, invasive species larvae remain trapped within internal motor systems. As boats traverse between water bodies, residual water releases these destructive organisms into pristine ecosystems.

Current Methods Fail

The "Clean, Drain, Dry" protocol depends on manual flushing. Yet 95% of boaters don't follow it when switching water bodies. Once established, invasive species are nearly impossible to eradicate.
